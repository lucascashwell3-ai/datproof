The grade measures proof, not promises.

Five pillars, 100 points, scored only from disclosed, dated, sourced facts — the rubric operationalizes what regulators already said about reserve reports and evidence. Current field: C D F. Anyone can re-run every grade from the public registry.

On-chain proof

40 pts

Published wallet addresses that reconcile against the live chain. All-or-nothing: existence is either independently checkable or it isn't. An A is impossible without it.

Disclosure quality

30 pts

Regulatory filings carry liability for misstatement; press releases don't. Scored from the registry's evidence tiers — filings earn full marks, statements partial, third-party attribution none.

Independent attestation

10 pts

A disclosed third-party check on custodian balances narrows the trust gap — credited, and capped, because the referees themselves warn it is not assurance.

Disclosure freshness

10 pts

A balance is a point-in-time claim that decays daily. Full marks inside 45 days, partial to 120, none beyond.

Balance-sheet resilience

10 pts

Fixed obligations serviced against a volatile asset thin the evidence's margin for error. Clean structure earns full marks.
